Sputtering targets

M²E (MoWTa Materials Enterprises) supplies a wide variety of sputtering targets & thin film deposition materials.Our sputtering targets come in a wide variation of sizes & materials, including pure elements or compounds & alloys in purities ranging from commercial grade to zone refined ultra pure grade. We supply most commonly requested shapes – round, rectangular, ring in more convenient sizes. If we do not have the shape you need, we can create a custom sputter targets order for you with any of the materials.

Sputtering is the thin film deposition manufacturing process at the heart of today’s semiconductors, disk drives, CDs, photovoltaic’s, flat panel displays and optical devices. The list below shows the range of materials we supply.
