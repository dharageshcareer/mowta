What We Supply

We supply materials like Molybdenum, Tungsten, Tungsten heavy metal, Tantalum, Niobium & Chromium for industries like coating industry, Electronics industry, Power sectors etc. we also supply thin film materials and semi finished goods such as sheets, wires, rods etc and basic parts/pieces for Furnace construction, Lamp components, coating systems, forming & machining tools etc.

We are here to supply fine Tungsten and Molybdenum wires of just 0.015 mm dia, which is almost invisible. They bear high voltages and cut even the hardest materials with absolute precision when used as electrodes in wire erosion machines. Our supply of fine wires can also be used as filaments in halogen lamps. We also supply boats for thermal evaporation and ion implantation systems in addition to twisted wires and coils.
